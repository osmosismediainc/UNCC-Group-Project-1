Background

The Journey To Our API Application

The Idea

Music is universal. Our team, team Beastmasters, decided we would make an API application to look up lyrics of music.

With whimsy, we invoke the spirit of Yoda from Star Wars. Yoda will translate the lyrics and provide the whimsy for our project

The APIs

AUDD.IO

A lot of developers use AudD to recognize music in their own applications. Also, they display the lyrics of the recognized songs or help users find lyrics by text.
