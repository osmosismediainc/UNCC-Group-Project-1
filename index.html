How To Use Our App

Welcome to Music App! Having trouble figuring out that song stuck in your head? Can you sing a little bit of it? If not, how about humming? You're at the right site!

To start, simply press record and hum or sing the song that's been driving you crazy. Save the recording, upload it to Music App and BOOM! Sanity restored
